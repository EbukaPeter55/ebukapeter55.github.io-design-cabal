About Design Cabal

From a design studio to a full-stack software powerhouse.

Design Cabal is a registered software company based in Nigeria, compliant with all local regulations and holding a valid certificate of incorporation. We are focused on building scalable digital products and bespoke software solutions for a global audience.

While we started as a design-focused studio, we have evolved into a full-stack software company with a strong product mindset and long-term vision. We combine aesthetic excellence with robust engineering to deliver software that scales.

We serve startups and enterprises globally, delivering world-class software solutions that compete on the international stage.

Long-Term Vision

Our goal extends beyond client services. We are building a portfolio of our own proprietary products and establishing subsidiaries to drive innovation across key sectors.

Registered & Compliant
